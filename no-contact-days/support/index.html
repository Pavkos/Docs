Help with No Contact: Days Since, subscriptions, hard date reminders, privacy questions and safety limitations.

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Not for Emergencies

This support page is not monitored for emergencies. If you may harm yourself, may harm someone else, feel unable to stay safe, or need urgent medical care, contact local emergency services, a crisis line, your clinician or a trusted person immediately.

General App Support

For non-urgent app issues, feature requests or bug reports, use the public GitHub issue tracker:

Open a support issue

Please do not include private journal text, sealed letters, contact logs, phone numbers, addresses, payment information or other sensitive personal data in public GitHub issues.

Subscriptions

No Contact Plus purchases are managed by Apple. To cancel or manage a subscription, open iOS Settings, tap your Apple ID, then Subscriptions. Purchase and refund questions are handled by Apple.

Hard Date Reminders

Hard date reminders are local notifications. You can turn them on or off in the app settings, and you can manage notification permissions in iOS Settings.

Privacy

Read the Privacy Policy. In the current version, app data is stored locally on device. Deleting the app removes local app data from that device.
