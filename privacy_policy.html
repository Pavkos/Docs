Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how StickerKick ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application StickerKick (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Camera data: The App requests access to your device camera solely to scan and identify football sticker cards. Photos are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers.
  • Usage data: We collect anonymous data about how you use the App (screens visited, features used, scan counts) via Amplitude analytics.
  • Purchase data: If you subscribe to StickerKick PRO, transaction data is processed by Apple App Store. We do not store your payment information.
  • Locally stored data: Your card collection and scan history are stored locally on your device using device storage. This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly share it.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide card scanning and price estimation features
  • To improve the App based on anonymous usage analytics
  • To process and restore your in-app subscription
  • To respond to your support requests

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • OpenAI (GPT-4o): Card images are sent to OpenAI for identification. OpenAI's privacy policy applies: openai.com/privacy
  • eBay Browse API / Apify: Card names are sent as text queries to retrieve price data. No personal data is shared.
  • Amplitude: Anonymous analytics. Amplitude's privacy policy: amplitude.com/privacy
  • Superwall: Manages in-app subscription paywalls. Superwall's privacy policy: superwall.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store: Handles all payment processing for subscriptions.

4. Data Retention

Card photos are not retained after processing. Usage analytics are retained in anonymised form. Your local collection data remains on your device until you delete the App.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: You may request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure: You may request deletion of your data.
  • Right to object: You may object to processing of your data.
  • Right to data portability: You may request transfer of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at ikorkod@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Your collection data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
