Privacy Policy

SpeachTutor · Last updated: 21 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SpeachTutor (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application for iOS (the “App”) and related services (together, the “Services”).

1. Information we collect

  • Account information. If you sign in with Apple, we receive identifiers and profile details that Apple shares with us (for example a user identifier and, if you choose, your name or email). Authentication and account storage are handled through our backend (Supabase).
  • Lesson and audio content. When you record or import spoken lessons, we process audio and derived text (transcripts) to provide analysis, exercises, and progress features you request.
  • Usage and device data. We may collect limited technical data such as app version, locale or language preference you set in the App, and diagnostic information needed to operate and secure the Services.
  • Purchase information. Subscription status is processed by Apple (StoreKit). We do not receive your full payment card details.

2. How we use information

We use the information above to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App (including transcription, AI-assisted lesson analysis, vocabulary, and practice features);
  • Authenticate you and sync your data across devices when you are signed in;
  • Communicate about the Services where appropriate;
  • Comply with law, enforce our terms, and protect rights and security.

3. AI and third-party services

To deliver the Services we use infrastructure and subprocessors, which may process your content on our instructions. These may include, depending on configuration:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and related cloud infrastructure;
  • Speech-to-text providers (e.g. Deepgram or similar) — to transcribe audio you submit;
  • Large language model providers (e.g. Anthropic Claude or similar) — to generate structured lesson analysis and coaching output from transcripts and related text;
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple, App Store, and subscription handling.

We select providers with appropriate safeguards, but their processing is also governed by their respective privacy policies.

4. Legal bases (EEA / UK users)

Where applicable, we rely on: performance of a contract (providing the Services); legitimate interests (security, improvement, analytics compatible with this Policy); consent where required (for example certain optional communications or non-essential cookies on any web properties); and legal obligation.

5. Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes above, unless a longer period is required by law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data as described below. Some information may remain in backups for a limited period before being overwritten.

6. International transfers

Our subprocessors may process data in countries other than your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses).

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise these rights, contact us using the email you publish in the “Operator” section above.

If you use Sign in with Apple, you can also use Apple’s controls to manage what is shared with apps.

8. Children

The Services are not directed at children under the age where parental consent is required in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from such children.

9. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and adjust the “Last updated” date. Material changes may require additional notice as required by law.
